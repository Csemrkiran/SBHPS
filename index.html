About Us

Shree Bhavani Higher Primary School, established in the year 2000 by Ramegowda C. C., is managed by Shree Bhavani Education Trust. Recognized by the Government of Karnataka, the school was founded to provide high-quality education to children in the rural region of Chikkanakana Hally, Mandya, Karnataka, aiming to help them achieve their academic and personal goals.

As a Co-educational Private Unaided institution, Shree Bhavani Higher Primary School offers a nurturing and inclusive environment where academic excellence and personal growth are paramount. The school is committed to supporting the overall development of its students through various activities and opportunities.

Key Highlights:

  • Recognized by the Government of Karnataka: The school is officially recognized for its quality education and adherence to state standards.
  • Committed to Rural Education: Shree Bhavani is dedicated to improving the quality of education in rural areas, ensuring that every child, regardless of their background, has access to learning and growth.
  • Extracurricular Activities: The school encourages participation in sports, cultural events, and science exhibitions, where students have excelled and brought several awards and recognition to the institution.
  • Supportive Environment: The teachers are known for being friendly and supportive, providing students with guidance and encouragement to thrive.
  • Transport Services: The school offers transportation facilities, making education accessible to students from nearby villages.

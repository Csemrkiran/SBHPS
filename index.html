About Us

Shree Bhavani Higher Primary School, established in the year 2000 by Ramegowda C. C., is managed by Shree Bhavani Education Trust. Recognized by the Government of Karnataka, the school was founded to provide high-quality education to children in the rural region of Chikkanakana Hally, Mandya, Karnataka, aiming to help them achieve their academic and personal goals.

As a Co-educational Private Unaided institution, Shree Bhavani Higher Primary School offers a nurturing and inclusive environment where academic excellence and personal growth are paramount. The school is committed to supporting the overall development of its students through various activities and opportunities.
